# Future Scope

## Short Term

- **Payment Gateway Integration** - Razorpay/Stripe for online payments
- **Product Reviews & Ratings** - User reviews with star ratings
- **Wishlist** - Save products for later
- **Dark Mode Toggle** - User-selectable theme switching
- **Image Upload** - Cloudinary integration for product images

## Medium Term

- **Email Notifications** - Order confirmation, shipping updates via Nodemailer
- **OAuth Authentication** - Google/GitHub login
- **Advanced Analytics** - Revenue charts, sales trends, popular products
- **Coupon & Discount System** - Promo codes, percentage/amount discounts
- **Advanced Search** - Full-text search with MongoDB Atlas Search

## Long Term

- **PWA Support** - Offline mode, push notifications, installable app
- **Multi-language Support** - i18n with react-intl or i18next
- **Mobile App** - React Native companion app
- **AI Recommendations** - Product recommendations based on browsing history
- **Multi-vendor Marketplace** - Allow third-party sellers
- **Real-time Chat** - Customer support with Socket.io
- **Inventory Management** - Low stock alerts, bulk import/export
- **Advanced Shipping** - Multiple shipping providers, tracking integration
